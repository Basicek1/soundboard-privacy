# Privacy Policy — SoundBoard Pro                                                                               
                                                                                                                  
  **Last updated: April 19, 2026**                                                                                
                                                                                                                  
  ## Data Collection                                                                                              
  SoundBoard Pro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.                                          
  All audio files and settings are stored locally on your device only.
                                                                                                                  
  ## Third-Party Services                                   
  The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
                                                                                                                  
  ## Permissions                          
  - **Media Library**: Used only to import audio files selected by the user.                                      
  - **Background Audio**: Used to continue playback when the app is in the background.
